Neymar JR

Neymar da Silva Santos Junior, known a Neymar, he is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a forward dor ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain and the Brazil national team. A prolific goalscorer and renowned playmaker, he is regarde as one of the best players in the world. Neymar has scored at least 100 goals for three different clubs, making him one of the three players to achive this.

Neymar came into prominence at Santos,where he made his professional debut aged 17. He helped the club win two successive Campeonato Paulista championship, a Copa do Brazil and the 2011 Copa Libertadors. Neymar was twice named the South American Footballer of the year, in 2011 and 2012. In 2018 , France Football ranked Neymar the world's third higest-paid footballer. The following year, Forbes ranked him the world's third highest-paid athlete.
